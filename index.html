The Take Away

Most courses "teach" you digital marketing, but lack execution. Some focus on execution, but on other company's data. (If I hear another problem Acme Corporation is facing, I'm going to scream.)

This course takes the best of both. Sorry, Acme.

In the course of 8 weeks, you will build a growth plan for the best company you know... yours!

Approachable Lessons

The course is taught in-person in NYC for 8 weeks, with two weeks of break.

Each lesson is 1.5 hours long, and includes a take-home assignment that you execute on your business. Each assignment can take an estimated 55 minutes - granted you paid full attention in class ;-)

Sample Lessons Include:

  • What to Grow? Defining Your Metrics
  • Crushing Your Paid Acquistion
  • Your 4-Prong Approach to SEO & SEM
  • Finding New Acquisition Channels
  • Retention: Your Business's Lifeline

Impactful Assignments

Each lesson is paired with an assignment that you will execute on your business, and see the impact of your learning in days.

Examples of assignments include:

  • Setting up an A/B test on your website to improve conversion
  • Measuring your customer data to identify your most valuable segments
  • Running a paid acquisition experiment to your target demo(s)
  • Developing virality and getting your top customers to refer their friends

Your results are then reviewed by a teaching staff of digital marketing veterans.

A Village of Support

Students will be invited to a Slack community group that includes current cohort members and program alumni.

Inside, students develop connections that extend well beyond the duration of the program.
